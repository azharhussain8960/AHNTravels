ASIA

Highlights: Cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, modern cities. Top Destinations: China (Great Wall), Japan (Tokyo), Thailand (Bangkok), India (Taj Mahal). Attractions: Temples, historical sites, bustling markets, tropical beaches.

AFRICA

Highlights: Safari adventures, ancient civilizations, diverse cultures. Top Destinations: Egypt (Pyramids of Giza), South Africa (Kruger National Park), Morocco (Marrakech). Attractions: Wildlife safaris, historical sites, vibrant markets, beaches.

SOUTH AMERICA

Highlights: Ancient ruins, natural beauty, vibrant cultures. Top Destinations: Brazil (Rio de Janeiro), Peru (Machu Picchu), Argentina (Buenos Aires). Attractions: Rainforests, archaeological sites, beaches, mountain ranges.

ANTARCTICA

Highlights: Pristine landscapes, unique wildlife, extreme adventures. Top Destinations: South Shetland Islands, Antarctic Peninsula, Ross Island. Attractions: Icebergs, penguin colonies, research stations, scenic cruises.

AUSTRALIA (and Oceania)

Highlights: Unique wildlife, stunning beaches, outdoor adventures. Top Destinations: Australia (Sydney, Great Barrier Reef), New Zealand (Queenstown). Attractions: Coral reefs, national parks, beaches, outdoor sports.

EUROPE

Highlights: Rich history, cultural landmarks, diverse cuisine. Top Destinations: France (Eiffel Tower), Italy (Rome), Greece (Santorini), UK (London). Attractions: Museums, historical monuments, scenic countryside, vibrant cities.

NOTRH AMERICA

Highlights: Natural wonders, entertainment, cultural diversity. Top Destinations: USA (New York City, Grand Canyon), Canada (Banff National Park), Mexico (Cancun). Attractions: National parks, theme parks, cultural festivals, historic sites.
