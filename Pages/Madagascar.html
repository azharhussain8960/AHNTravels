Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Antananarivo

Antananarivo, the capital city of Madagascar, is known for its historical landmarks and vibrant culture. The city offers attractions such as the Rova of Antananarivo, the Andafiavaratra Palace, and the bustling Analakely Market.

Toamasina

Toamasina, also known as Tamatave, is a major port city on the east coast of Madagascar. The city offers attractions such as the beautiful beaches of Foulpointe, the Ivoloina Park, and the bustling Bazary Be Market.

Antsirabe

Antsirabe, located in the central highlands of Madagascar, is known for its thermal springs and colonial architecture. The city offers attractions such as the Thermal Baths, the Cathedral of Antsirabe, and the scenic Lake Tritriva.

Mahajanga

Mahajanga, a coastal city in northwestern Madagascar, is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife. The city offers attractions such as the Cirque Rouge, the Mahajanga Promenade, and the nearby Anjajavy Forest Reserve.

Fianarantsoa

Fianarantsoa, a city in the central highlands, is known for its historical significance and beautiful landscapes. The city offers attractions such as the Old Town, the Fianarantsoa Cathedral, and the surrounding vineyards.

Toliara

Toliara, also known as Tulear, is a coastal city in southwestern Madagascar known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture. The city offers attractions such as the Arboretum d'Antsokay, the Ifaty Beach, and the Tsimanampetsotsa National Park.

Nosy Be

Nosy Be, an island off the northwest coast of Madagascar, is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant marine life. The island offers attractions such as the Lokobe National Park, the Nosy Be Beach, and the nearby Nosy Komba Island. .

Antsiranana

Antsiranana, also known as Diego Suarez, is a city in northern Madagascar known for its natural beauty and colonial architecture. The city offers attractions such as the Diego Suarez Bay, the Amber Mountain National Park, and the French colonial buildings.

Morondava

Morondava, a coastal city in western Madagascar, is known for its unique landscapes and vibrant culture. The city offers attractions such as the Avenue of the Baobabs, the Kirindy Forest Reserve, and the Belo Sur Mer beach.

Sambava

Sambava, a city in northeastern Madagascar, is known for its lush landscapes and vanilla production. The city offers attractions such as the Marojejy National Park, the Antsiranana Falls, and the beautiful beaches of Sambava.
