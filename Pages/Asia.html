Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

India

Home of the iconic Taj Mahal, Known as the "Pink City" for its trademark building color.India is generally budget-friendly.

nepal

City-state known for its modern skyline, efficient public transport, and multicultural heritage, Famous for Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and the iconic Merlion statue,Resort island with beaches, theme parks, and attractions like Universal Studios Singapore, Singapore is one of the more expensive destinations in Asia.

japan

Modern metropolis with vibrant nightlife and technology hubs, Famous for its classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. trademark building color.Japan can be relatively expensive for travelers.

Thailand

Capital city known for ornate shrines and vibrant street life, Famous for its beaches, islands, and nightlife, Northern city known for its beautiful temples and mountainous surroundings, Thailand offers a range of options from budget to luxury.

Vietnam

Capital known for its centuries-old architecture and a rich culture with Southeast Asian, Chinese, and French influences,Formerly Saigon, a city known for its pivotal role in the Vietnam War. Ha Long Bay: UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its emerald waters and thousands of towering limestone islands topped with rainforests.

United Arab Emirates (UAE)

Known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and a lively nightlife scene, UAE's capital, known for modern skyscrapers and shopping megacenters, UAE can be expensive, particularly in Dubai.

indonesia

Famous for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs, Capital city known for its mix of cultures, from Javanese to Arab, Indian, and European, Cultural hub with a rich heritage of temples and palaces, Indonesia is generally affordable.

china

Capital city known for its modern architecture, historic sites like the Forbidden City and Tiananmen Square, Global financial hub with a futuristic skyline and historic Bund waterfront area, Ancient capital known for the Terracotta Army and ancient city walls, : China varies widely in cost depending on the region.

South Korea

Capital city known for its modern skyscrapers, K-pop culture, and historic sites like Gyeongbokgung Palace, Coastal city known for its beaches, mountains, and temples, South Korea can be moderately expensive.

malaysia

Capital city known for its modern skyline dominated by the Petronas Twin Towers, Island known for its beaches, colonial architecture, and multicultural heritage,Archipelago of islands known for its beaches, rainforests, and diverse wildlife Malaysia is generally affordable.

sri lanka

Capital city known for its colonial heritage and vibrant markets, Island known for its beaches, colonial architecture, and multicultural heritage,Home to the Temple of the Tooth, a sacred Buddhist site, Coastal city known for its Dutch colonial buildings and fortifications, Sri Lanka is quite affordable.

Singapore

City-state known for its modern skyline, efficient public transport, and multicultural heritage, Famous for Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and the iconic Merlion statue,Resort island with beaches, theme parks, and attractions like Universal Studios Singapore, Singapore is one of the more expensive destinations in Asia.
