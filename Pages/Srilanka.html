Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Colombo

Colombo, the capital city of Sri Lanka, is known for its vibrant culture, colonial architecture, and bustling markets. Major attractions include the Galle Face Green, Colombo National Museum, Gangaramaya Temple, and Pettah Market. The city offers a mix of modern urban life and historic charm.

Kandy

Kandy is a historic city known for its scenic beauty and cultural significance. Key attractions include the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, Kandy Lake, Peradeniya Botanical Gardens, and the annual Esala Perahera festival. The city offers a serene environment with rich cultural heritage.

Galle

Galle is a coastal city known for its well-preserved Dutch colonial architecture and historic fort. Popular attractions include Galle Fort, the Lighthouse, Unawatuna Beach, and the National Maritime Museum. The city offers a charming blend of history, culture, and scenic beauty.

Sigiriya

Sigiriya is known for its ancient rock fortress, also called Lion Rock, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Key attractions include the Sigiriya Rock Fortress, frescoes, and the Royal Gardens. The city offers a glimpse into Sri Lanka's ancient history and architectural marvels.

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, also known as "Little England," is famous for its cool climate, tea plantations, and colonial-era architecture. Popular attractions include Horton Plains National Park, Victoria Park, and the scenic Gregory Lake. The city offers a picturesque retreat with lush landscapes and tea estates.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is an ancient city known for its well-preserved ruins of the Sri Lankan civilization. Major attractions include the Sacred Bo Tree (Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi), Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, and the ancient monasteries. The city offers a profound historical and spiritual experience.

Jaffna

Jaffna is known for its unique Tamil culture, historic sites, and vibrant markets. Key attractions include the Jaffna Fort, Nallur Kandaswamy Temple, Jaffna Public Library, and Casuarina Beach. The city offers a rich cultural experience with distinct northern Sri Lankan heritage.

Trincomalee

Trincomalee is a coastal city known for its beautiful beaches, natural harbors, and historic temples. Popular attractions include Nilaveli Beach, Pigeon Island National Park, Koneswaram Temple, and Fort Frederick. The city offers a blend of natural beauty, history, and cultural sites.

Ella

Ella is known for its breathtaking landscapes, tea plantations, and hiking trails. Major attractions include the Nine Arches Bridge, Ella Rock, Little Adam's Peak, and Ravana Falls. The city offers a serene environment with opportunities for outdoor activities and scenic views.

Bentota

Bentota is a coastal city known for its stunning beaches, water sports, and luxury resorts. Key attractions include Bentota Beach, Brief Garden, and the turtle hatcheries. The city offers a relaxing getaway with opportunities for water activities and beachside relaxation.
