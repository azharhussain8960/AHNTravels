Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Brazil

Rio de Janeiro Iconic city known for Copacabana Beach, Christ the Redeemer statue, and Carnival Amazon Rainforest: World's largest tropical rainforest with diverse wildlife and eco-tourism opportunities,Stunning waterfall system on the border with Argentina Brazil can vary widely in cost.

Argentina

Buenos Aires: Capital city with European-style architecture, tango culture, and vibrant nightlife, Southern region with glaciers, mountains, and wildlife Spectacular waterfalls shared with Brazil, Argentina is generally affordable.

Peru

Machu Picchu: Ancient Inca city in the Andes Mountains, a UNESCO World Heritage Site,Capital known for its colonial architecture and culinary scene. Region with Inca ruins and traditional Andean villages.Peru is relatively affordable.

Chile

Capital city surrounded by the Andes Mountains, known for museums and wine regions , Remote island with Moai statues and Polynesian culture, World's driest desert with unique landscapes and starry skies Chile can be moderately expensive.

Colombia

Colonial city on the Caribbean coast known for its architecture and nearby beaches, Capital city with museums, historic neighborhoods, and nearby Andean peaks, Modern city with a pleasant climate, cultural attractions, and surrounding mountains Colombia is generally affordable.

Ecuador

Unique wildlife and landscapes that inspired Darwin's theory of evolution, Capital city in the Andes Mountains with a well-preserved colonial center, Diverse ecosystem with indigenous communities and wildlife Ecuador is generally affordable.

Bolivia

World's largest salt flat with surreal landscapes and flamingo-filled lakes, High-altitude capital city with markets, colonial architecture, and nearby mountains, Highest navigable lake with traditional cultures and floating islands Bolivia is one of the more affordable destinations.

Uruguay

Capital city known for its modern architecture, historic sites like the Forbidden City and Tiananmen Square, Global financial hub with a futuristic skyline and historic Bund waterfront area, Ancient capital known for the Terracotta Army and ancient city walls, : China varies widely in cost depending on the region.

Venezuela

Tallest waterfall in the world, located in Canaima National Park, Capital city with museums, historic buildings, and a cultural scene, Caribbean islands with coral reefs and turquoise waters Due to economic instability, travel to Venezuela is currently challenging and costly. Costs can vary widely.

Paraguay

Capital city known for colonial architecture and lively markets, Border city known for shopping and proximity to Iguazu Falls, Hydroelectric dam on the Paraná River, one of the world's largest, Paraguay is generally affordable.

Suriname

The capital city, known for its Dutch colonial architecture and vibrant markets, Offers hiking trails and wildlife spotting,A UNESCO World Heritage site with rich biodiversity Known for its plantation history and boat tours.

French Guiana

The capital city, known for its Creole architecture, bustling markets, and botanical gardens. Famous for the former penal colony, including Devil's Island, and now a historical and tourist site. Europe's spaceport, offering guided tours and the opportunity to witness rocket launches.
