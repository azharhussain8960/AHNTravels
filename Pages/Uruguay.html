Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Montevideo

Montevideo, the capital city of Uruguay, is known for its eclectic blend of colonial architecture, modern skyscrapers, and beautiful beaches. The city features the historic Ciudad Vieja district, the bustling Mercado del Puerto, and the scenic Rambla, a waterfront promenade.

Punta del Este

Punta del Este is a glamorous beach resort town known for its luxury resorts, upscale shopping, and vibrant nightlife. The city offers stunning beaches such as Playa Brava and Playa Mansa, the iconic sculpture La Mano, and a lively marina.

Colonia del Sacramento

Colonia del Sacramento, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is famous for its charming cobblestone streets, colonial architecture, and historical significance. The city's Barrio Histórico features well-preserved buildings, the picturesque Plaza Mayor, and the historic lighthouse.

Salto

Salto, located in northwestern Uruguay, is known for its hot springs, historic architecture, and natural beauty. The city offers attractions such as the Termas del Daymán hot springs, the picturesque Plaza Artigas, and the nearby Salto Grande Dam.

Piriápolis

Piriápolis is a charming coastal town known for its beautiful beaches, historic buildings, and scenic hills. The city features the historic Argentino Hotel, the picturesque Cerro San Antonio with its panoramic views, and the lively Rambla de los Argentinos.

Carmelo

Carmelo, situated in southwestern Uruguay, is known for its vineyards, serene beaches, and tranquil ambiance. The city offers attractions such as the scenic Playa Seré, the historic Calera de las Huérfanas, and the numerous local wineries.

Maldonado

Maldonado, located near Punta del Este, is known for its historical sites, beautiful coastline, and vibrant culture. The city features the impressive Catedral de San Fernando, the historic Cuartel de Dragones, and the nearby Playa Mansa.

La Paloma

La Paloma is a laid-back beach town known for its stunning coastline, relaxed atmosphere, and excellent surfing conditions. The city offers beautiful beaches such as Playa La Balconada, the historic Faro de La Paloma lighthouse, and a variety of outdoor activities.
