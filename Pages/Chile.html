Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Santiago

Santiago, the capital city of Chile, is known for its modern urban environment nestled between the Andes mountains and the Pacific Ocean. The city offers a mix of historic sites, such as Plaza de Armas and La Moneda Palace.

Valparaíso

Valparaíso, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is renowned for its colorful hillside homes, vibrant street art, and historic funiculars. The city's coastal setting and unique architecture make it a popular destination.

Punta Arenas

Punta Arenas, located in southern Chile, is a gateway to the Antarctic and the stunning landscapes of Patagonia. The city features landmarks such as the Magellan Strait, the Nao Victoria Museum, and the historic Sara Braun Palace.

San Pedro de Atacama

San Pedro de Atacama is a small town in northern Chile, known for its otherworldly desert landscapes and archaeological sites. Visitors can explore the Valle de la Luna.

Valdivia

Valdivia is a charming city in southern Chile, situated at the confluence of several rivers. Known for its rich history and German heritage, the city offers attractions such as the Valdivia Fort System, the coastal reserve of Punta Curiñanco, and the bustling riverside markets.

Puerto Varas

Puerto Varas, also known as the "City of Roses," is located in the Chilean Lake District. The city is famous for its German-influenced architecture, stunning views of the Osorno and Calbuco volcanoes, and outdoor activities.

Iquique

Iquique, situated on Chile's northern coast, is known for its beautiful beaches, historic architecture, and duty-free shopping. The city offers a blend of relaxation and adventure.

La Serena

La Serena is one of Chile's oldest cities, known for its long sandy beaches and well-preserved colonial architecture. The city features attractions such as the La Recova Market, the historic Plaza de Armas, and numerous churches..
