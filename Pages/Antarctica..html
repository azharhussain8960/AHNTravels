Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Argentina

Antarctic Peninsula, South Shetland Islands,Ushuaia, the southernmost city in the world, is a popular departure point for Antarctic cruises. Visitors can explore Tierra del Fuego National Park and the Beagle Channel before embarking on their journey.

Salta

Salta, located in northwest Argentina, is known for its colonial architecture, rich cultural heritage, and stunning natural landscapes. The city offers attractions such as the historic Plaza 9 de Julio, the San Francisco Church and Convent, and the scenic Lerma Valley.

New Zealand

Christchurch offers access to the Ross Sea region of Antarctica. The city is known for its beautiful gardens, parks, and vibrant arts scene.

Australia

Hobart is the capital of Tasmania and serves as a gateway to East Antarctica. The city is known for its maritime history, arts, and culinary delights.

South Africa

Cape Town offers expeditions to Queen Maud Land and nearby sub-Antarctic islands. The city is famous for its Table Mountain, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

Norway

Norway has a rich history of polar exploration, with legendary explorers such as Roald Amundsen. Travelers often depart from Oslo, connecting through cities like Punta Arenas or Ushuaia. Oslo offers a range of cultural and historical attractions, including museums dedicated to polar exploration.

France

RÃ©union Island, a French overseas department in the Indian Ocean, is sometimes used as a departure point for scientific missions to Antarctica. The island is known for its volcanic landscapes, lush forests, and rich Creole culture, providing a fascinating starting point for Antarctic expeditions.

United Kingdom

The Falkland Islands, a British Overseas Territory, serve as a gateway to Antarctica, particularly for cruises. The capital, Stanley, offers a unique blend of British culture and rugged natural beauty, with opportunities to see diverse wildlife and explore historical sites.

Chile

Located in southern Chile, Punta Arenas is a significant departure point for both flights and cruises to Antarctica. The city is rich in history and offers cultural experiences, including museums and historical sites.

Ushuaia

Ushuaia, located at the southern tip of Argentina, is known as the "End of the World." It offers breathtaking landscapes, including the Beagle Channel and Tierra del Fuego National Park. Ushuaia is a gateway to Antarctic cruises, with opportunities for hiking, skiing, and exploring the region's unique wildlife and indigenous culture.
