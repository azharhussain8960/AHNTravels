Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Hanoi

Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam, is known for its rich history, French colonial architecture, and vibrant street life. Popular attractions include Hoan Kiem Lake, the Old Quarter, Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, and the Temple of Literature. The city also offers a variety of museums, cultural sites, and traditional Vietnamese cuisine.

Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City, formerly known as Saigon, is Vietnam's largest city and a bustling metropolis. Key attractions include the War Remnants Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica of Saigon, Ben Thanh Market, and the Cu Chi Tunnels. The city is known for its lively nightlife, shopping districts, and diverse food scene.

Hoi An

Hoi An is a charming ancient town known for its well-preserved architecture, lantern-lit streets, and rich cultural heritage. Popular sites include the Japanese Covered Bridge, Hoi An Ancient Town, and the vibrant night markets. The city is also famous for its tailor shops, traditional crafts, and nearby beaches.

Hue

Hue, the former imperial capital of Vietnam, is known for its historic sites and royal heritage. Key attractions include the Imperial City, Thien Mu Pagoda, and the Royal Tombs. The city is also renowned for its traditional Vietnamese cuisine, particularly dishes that were once served to the royal family.

Da Nang

Da Nang is a coastal city known for its sandy beaches, modern architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Major attractions include My Khe Beach, Marble Mountains, Dragon Bridge, and the Ba Na Hills with the famous Golden Bridge. The city serves as a gateway to the ancient town of Hoi An and the imperial city of Hue.

Nha Trang

Nha Trang is a coastal resort city known for its beautiful beaches, clear waters, and diving sites. Popular attractions include the Vinpearl Amusement Park, Po Nagar Cham Towers, and the Nha Trang Cathedral. The city is also famous for its seafood, vibrant nightlife, and luxury resorts.

Sapa

Sapa is a mountainous town known for its stunning terraced rice fields, ethnic minority communities, and trekking opportunities. Key attractions include Fansipan Mountain, Muong Hoa Valley, and the Sapa Stone Church. The town offers a cool climate and is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Halong Bay

Halong Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its emerald waters, limestone karsts, and thousands of islands and islets. Popular activities include boat cruises, kayaking, and exploring caves such as Sung Sot Cave and Thien Cung Cave. The bay offers stunning natural scenery and is a must-visit destination in Vietnam.

Phu Quoc

Phu Quoc is a tropical island known for its pristine beaches, clear waters, and lush forests. Major attractions include Long Beach, Sao Beach, and the Vinpearl Safari. The island is also famous for its fish sauce, pearl farms, and luxury resorts, making it a popular destination for relaxation and water activities.

Can Tho

Can Tho is known as the heart of the Mekong Delta and is famous for its floating markets, such as Cai Rang Floating Market. Key attractions include the Can Tho Bridge, Binh Thuy Ancient House, and the beautiful Ninh Kieu Wharf. The city offers a glimpse into the riverine life and rich agricultural heritage of the delta region.
