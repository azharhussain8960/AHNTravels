Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Auckland

Auckland, the largest city in New Zealand, is known for its stunning harbors, multicultural vibe, and iconic landmarks. The city offers attractions such as the Sky Tower, Auckland War Memorial Museum, and the beautiful waterfront Viaduct Harbour.

Hamilton

Hamilton, located in the Waikato region, is known for its lush gardens, vibrant arts scene, and nearby natural attractions. The city offers attractions such as the Hamilton Gardens, the Waikato Museum, and the Hamilton Zoo.

Wellington

Wellington, the capital city of New Zealand, is known for its vibrant arts scene, stunning harbor views, and compact city center. The city offers attractions such as Te Papa Tongarewa (the national museum), Zealandia eco-sanctuary, and the historic Wellington Cable Car.

Christchurch

Christchurch, on New Zealand's South Island, is known for its English heritage, botanical gardens, and post-earthquake rebuilding efforts. The city offers attractions such as the Christchurch Botanic Gardens, the Canterbury Museum, and the scenic Avon River punting.

Queenstown

Queenstown, nestled on Lake Wakatipu in the Southern Alps, is known for its adventure sports, stunning scenery, and vibrant nightlife. The city offers attractions such as skiing at The Remarkables, bungee jumping at Kawarau Bridge, and cruising on Milford Sound.

Rotorua

Rotorua, located in the Bay of Plenty region, is known for its geothermal wonders, Maori culture, and adventure activities. The city offers attractions such as Te Puia geothermal park, Wai-O-Tapu Thermal Wonderland, and traditional Maori cultural experiences.

Dunedin

Dunedin, on New Zealand's South Island, is known for its Scottish heritage, Victorian architecture, and vibrant student population. The city offers attractions such as Larnach Castle, Otago Peninsula wildlife tours, and the historic Dunedin Railway Station.

Napier

Napier, in the Hawke's Bay region, is known for its Art Deco architecture, vineyards, and Mediterranean climate. The city offers attractions such as the National Aquarium of New Zealand, Hawke's Bay wine tours, and the annual Art Deco Festival.
