Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Lima

Lima, the capital city of Peru, is known for its rich history and culinary excellence. The city features impressive colonial architecture, museums such as the Larco Museum, and vibrant neighborhoods like Miraflores and Barranco.

Cusco

Cusco, a city in the Peruvian Andes, was once the capital of the Inca Empire. Known for its archaeological remains and Spanish colonial architecture, Cusco is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Arequipa

Arequipa, known as the "White City" due to its stunning white volcanic stone buildings, is surrounded by three impressive volcanoes. The city's historic center is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Trujillo

Trujillo, located on the northern coast of Peru, is known for its colonial architecture, ancient ruins, and vibrant cultural festivals. The city is close to the archaeological sites of Chan Chan and the Temples of the Sun and Moon.

Iquitos

Iquitos, the largest city in the Peruvian Amazon, is only accessible by boat or plane. Known for its lush rainforest and river tours, Iquitos offers unique experiences such as exploring the Amazon River.

Puno

Puno, located on the shores of Lake Titicaca, is known for its traditional Andean culture and folklore. The city serves as a gateway to exploring the world's highest navigable lake and the unique Uros floating islands.

Nazca

Nazca is famous for the mysterious Nazca Lines, ancient geoglyphs etched into the desert floor. Visitors can take aerial tours to view these enormous figures, which include shapes of animals, plants, and geometric patterns.

Huaraz

Huaraz, located in the heart of the Peruvian Andes, is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Known for its stunning mountain scenery, Huaraz offers access to the Huascarán National Park, home to the highest peaks in Peru.
