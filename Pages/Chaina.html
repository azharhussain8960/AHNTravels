Visit Your Favorite Country With AHN Global Travels

Beijing

Beijing, the capital city of China, is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. Major attractions include the Great Wall of China, the Forbidden City, Tiananmen Square, and the Summer Palace. The city offers a blend of ancient architecture, modern skyscrapers, and traditional Chinese culture.

Shanghai

Shanghai is a global financial hub known for its modern skyline, historic landmarks, and vibrant culture. Popular attractions include The Bund, Oriental Pearl Tower, Yu Garden, and the Shanghai Museum. The city offers a mix of contemporary architecture, traditional markets, and a lively nightlife.

Xi'an

Xi'an is famous for its historical significance as the starting point of the Silk Road and the home of the Terracotta Army. Key attractions include the Terracotta Warriors, Xi'an City Wall, Big Wild Goose Pagoda, and the Muslim Quarter. The city offers a rich blend of ancient Chinese history and diverse cultural influences.

Guangzhou

Guangzhou, also known as Canton, is known for its vibrant economy, cultural heritage, and delicious cuisine. Major attractions include the Canton Tower, Chen Clan Ancestral Hall, Shamian Island, and the Temple of the Six Banyan Trees. The city is famous for its Cantonese food, bustling markets, and historical sites.

Chengdu

Chengdu is known for its relaxed lifestyle, rich history, and being the home of the giant panda. Popular attractions include the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding, Jinli Ancient Street, Wuhou Shrine, and Mount Qingcheng. The city is also famous for its spicy Sichuan cuisine and tea houses.

Guilin

Guilin is renowned for its stunning karst landscapes, picturesque rivers, and lush greenery. Key attractions include the Li River, Reed Flute Cave, Elephant Trunk Hill, and the Longji Rice Terraces. The city offers a serene environment with opportunities for river cruises, hiking, and exploring natural wonders.

Hangzhou

Hangzhou is known for its scenic beauty, historic sites, and cultural heritage. Major attractions include West Lake, Lingyin Temple, the Grand Canal, and the tea plantations of Longjing. The city offers a peaceful retreat with beautiful gardens, pagodas, and a rich history dating back thousands of years.

Suzhou

Suzhou is known for its classical gardens, historic canals, and traditional architecture. Popular sites include the Humble Administrator's Garden, Lingering Garden, Suzhou Museum, and the ancient water town of Tongli. The city offers a glimpse into traditional Chinese garden design and a serene atmosphere.

Lhasa

Lhasa, the capital of Tibet, is known for its spiritual significance and stunning mountain landscapes. Key attractions include the Potala Palace, Jokhang Temple, Barkhor Street, and Norbulingka. The city offers a unique blend of Tibetan culture, Buddhist monasteries, and breathtaking views of the Himalayas.

Hong Kong

Hong Kong is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic skyline, vibrant nightlife, and diverse culture. Major attractions include Victoria Peak, Disneyland, the Big Buddha on Lantau Island, and the bustling markets of Mong Kok. The city offers a dynamic mix of modernity and tradition, with world-class dining and shopping.
